FAQs

Q. How can I talk with a recruiter?     

A. Call us at 704 544 9000 between 8am and 5PM ET Monday through Friday

Q. What kind of benefits are available through Allied Technical Resources?     

A. Allied Technical Resources offers our employees a robust benefits package with 401(k) plan, healthcare insurance including dental and life insurance. You are entitled to participate in any and all offerings for which you meet the eligibility requirements as an Allied Technical Resources employee. Upon hiring, you will receive a detailed package outlining the benefits available to you.

Q. What type of employment options are available?     

A. Our customer’s needs determine the options for a particular assignment and they may be temporary, temporary to permanent or direct placement:
• A temporary position is typically a limited duration opportunity, often based around a project, which may range from several months to a year or even longer.
• A temporary to permanent position is an opportunity that will lead to a permanent placement if the customer is satisfied with the employees performance over a short duration of time. The individual works as an employee of Allied Technical Resources, Inc. until he/she is offered a position and therefor will become an employee of the customer.
• A direct placement opportunity is a full time position with one of our customers. The individual is recruited by Allied Technical Resources, Inc., but works as an employee of the customer.

Q. Am I charged a fee when you find me a job?     

A. Our customers compensate us to find them top quality talent so there is no fee charged to the person looking for a job.

Q. How can I talk with a recruiter?     

A. Call us at 704 544 9000 between 8am and 5PM EST Monday through Friday

Q. How often will I get paid?     

A. Allied Technical Resources, Inc. normal payroll schedule is on a weekly basis for all hours worked. However, payroll schedules may vary based on the customer. We offer direct deposit and paper checks.

Q. If I don’t see a current open job posting that interest me, can I submit my resume to Allied Technical Resources, Inc. for future consideration?     

A. Call us at 704 544 9000 between 8am and 5PM EST Monday through Friday

Q. How can I talk with a recruiter?     

A. Individuals can submit their resume on our Submit Resume “button”. The resume will be stored in our database and available to our recruiters for future opportunities. A recruiter will reach out to you if you are considered a match. Click here: Submit Resume
