RECRUITMENT SERVICES

Talent you want. Solutions you need.

Temporary & Temporary-to-Permanent Staffing

Allied Technical Resources, Inc. places temporary employees to clients for a duration based on your needs. An employer who selects ATR temporary personnel does so for a variety of reasons, including:

• Allows the flexibility of staffing for peak work loads without having to make the commitment of hiring direct permanent employees.

• Allows a shift in the cost in the staffing process by outsourcing the costs of recruiting, on boarding, and paying workers.

• Allows a “try before you buy” evaluation an employee’s skills and corporate culture fit before making a hire.

As the temporary worker’s employer, ATR is responsible for paying or deducting all federal, state and local taxes, as well as covering the employee with workers compensation, liability and unemployment compensation insurance.


Direct Placement

Allied Technical Resources, Inc. also assists our clients in direct hire staffing and placement. We have a proven search process and experienced recruiters who will identify and screen candidates to find that perfect fit for your direct hire needs.

PAYROLL SERVICES

Flexible, customized and low cost

Allied Technical Resources, Inc. offers flexible, customized, and low cost payroll services delivered by an experienced and talented team of Human Resources professionals who will quickly respond to your requests. Allied Technical Resources, Inc. is sensitive to co-employment issues facing today's corporations.


Our Payroll Services team is flexible, creative, and understands your business. They will work with you to customize a plan to meet your on-boarding, off-boarding and payroll processing needs.


It is our goal to identify and place candidates in a timely manner that satisfies contract requirements. We are experts in multi-state payroll processing, per diem administration, and contractor employment. Allied Technical Resources, Inc. processes payroll in-house with flexible cycles and offers direct deposit.


By serving as the employer of record for contractors, we assume the human resources risk for payroll and legal/regulatory compliance, making it simpler for companies to access the staff they need without concern about financial liability.
