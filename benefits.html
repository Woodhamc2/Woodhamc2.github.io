Employee Benefits

What’s in it for you?

  • Insurance

          Long and Short Term Disability - Short Term and Long Term Disability plans provide financial protection for you by paying a portion of your income when you are disabled. The amount you receive is based on the amount you earned before your disability began.

          Life Insurance - The life insurance plan provides financial protection for your beneficiary (ies) by paying a benefit in the event of your death. The amount that your beneficiary (ies) receive(s) is based on the amount of coverage in effect just prior to the date of your death according to the terms and provisions of the plan. You also have the opportunity to have coverage for your dependents.

          Universal Whole Life - offered during the next Open Enrollment period

          Accidental Coverage - offered during the next Open Enrollment period

          Critical Illness - offered during the next Open Enrollment period

  • Health

          Dental Insurance - Employees have a choice of Dental Insurance that covers diagnostic and preventative care as well as a percentage of restorative and surgical care.

          Medical Health Insurance -Allied Technical Resources, Inc. provides a choice of medical insurance plans and hospital indemnity for our employees. Employees are given a choice of three different levels of health insurance coverage. The choices of plans range from a minimum coverage plan to a co-pay plan with a low deductible. A hospital indemnity plan pays a benefit whenever you or your covered family members are admitted to the hospital. Whether you’re being treated on an inpatient or outpatient basis, this coverage can help you manage your expenses.

  • Finance

          Flexible Spending Account - A Section 125 Health Care Flexible Spending Account helps pay the employee’s out-of-pocket medical costs while increasing spendable income. Under this plan, a specified amount of pre-taxed money is deducted from the employee’s paycheck each pay period. These dollars are set-aside in a reimbursement account from which the employee can draw in order to pay for qualified health care expenses.

          Weekly pay - Allied Technical Resources, Inc. normal payroll schedule is on a weekly basis for all hours worked. However, payroll schedules may vary based on the customer.

          We offer direct deposit or paper checks.

          BB&T Banking discounts - BB&T offer special discounts for Allied Technical Resources, Inc. employees.

          Verizon Wireless Employee discount - Verizon offers a corporate discount for Allied Technical Resources Employees.
